What is Manga?

Manga is a style of Japanese graphic novels through illustrated comics, with expressive art, dynamic characters and action-packed stories. It has become available for readers all around the world of all ages. There are various genres and styles to explore; from action, romance, horror, and slice of life. Allowing it to be accessible and enjoyable for a wide range of readers. Here at Manga Match we are here to help you find that perfect read. Be it your first time reading manga or you are a seasoned fan, there is always somehting new and exciting to discover.

  • Start from the right side of the page.
  • Read the panels from right to left.
  • Follow the flow of the speach bubbles from right to left and top to bottom.

How to Read Manga?

If you are just starting out with manga it can be a bit of a challenge. But worry not! We have a step-by-step guide to help you get started. Unlike western comics, manga is read from right to left. As well as the front of the book being the last page of the book, it is the opposite with manga.

Why Read Manga?

Engaging Stories

Manga creates a deep and immersive storytelling and character development that keeps readers engaged from start to finish.

Variety of Genres

There is a wide arrange of manga genres to explore, including action, adventure, romance, horror, and slice of life. There is a story for everyone.

Visually Engaging

The visual storytelling and artistic detail create a unique visually engaging reading experience for new and experienced readers alike.
