What is Manga?

Manga is a style of Japanese graphic novels through illustrated comics, with expressive art, dynamic characters and action-packed stories. With the wide variety of manga it can be overwhelming and difficult to find that perfect read. Well, worry no longer! Here at Manga Match we have a variety of manga sorted by genres sorted by diffent levels. Be it you're just starting to learn how to read manga, or you're a seasoned reader looking for something new and trending. Explore our many different options.
