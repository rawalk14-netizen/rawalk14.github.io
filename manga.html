Trending Manga to Explore

Witch Hat Atelier

Witch Hat Atelier is a magical adventure manga that follows the story of a young girl names Coco who dreams about becoming a witch. But one can only become a witch if they were born one...or so she thought until she spies upon Quifrey performing magic through artistic illustrations. Unknowing she uses forbidden magic, trapping her mother within crystal. Coco then sets out on a journey to learn the proper ways of magic in hopes to save her mother.

Spy x Family

Spy x Family is a action packed manga with a side of comedy that follows a spy named Twilight who must form a fake family to complete a top secret mission in saving his country. He forms a fake family with Anya, a young girl with telepathic abilities that she keeps secret. As well as Yor, who is posing as his wife to hide her own secret identity of being an assasin. Along the way these three grow closer as a family. Twilight struggles with forming true connections with others, he comes face to face with his inner conflicts of "is it for the mission?" or "is it for my family?"

One Piece

One Piece is a long and beloved series among many manga fans. As an ongoing series, it follows the story of Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew. Together they travel the seas in search of the "One Piece". A treasure that is one of a kind and promise of unimaginable wealth. Along the way they save people from malicious pirates and overcome many difficult challenges. This adventure is filled with silly, but also heartfelt moments that captivate readers of all ages.
